Tutoring Schedule

Creating a Session

1. Go to your Google Calendar and log in as the same email as this site.

2. Create an event and fill in the appropriate location and times

3. Invite helpmehelpmetutor@gmail.com to the event

> Our applet listener will add your session to the central calendar here

Deleting a Session

1. Click on the event you want to delete in the calendar.

2. Click 'more details>>'

3. Remove yourself from the event

> The session will be dropped from the calendar and attending students will be notified.
